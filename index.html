About


I'm a front end developer residing in the great city of Los Angeles. I enjoy developing websites and making ideas come to life through the power of code!


I attended college for some years with a focus on computer science but eventually found passion for the web. I decided to continue my education as a self taught developer using various resources from books, tutorials, coding sites and anything I could find. Some of the sites I've used are TreeHouse, CodeAcademy and FreeCodeCamp.

FreeCodeCamp Front-End Certificate


Some of my technical skills include HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery and a bit of AngularJs. Grunt likes to help me out occassionally, Sass, well... makes my CSS sassy! while my guardian angel Git is always watching over me.


When I'm not coding or learning new technologies I enjoy various activities. I like playing video games, specially Blizzard's games (WoW and Diablo anyone!?). I also enjoy hiking and being one with nature. Finally, one of my all time favorites is bowling; there's nothing like bashing pins after spending hours trying to figure out what's wrong with your program!
